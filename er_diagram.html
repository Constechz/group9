👥 Core Entities

  • Students Holds personal info, national identification, and driving permit status.
  • Instructors Stores information about teachers, license classes, and contact info.
  • Managers Tracks administrative staff managing operations and registrations.

📝 Operations

  • Registrations Links a student to courses, tracking costs, deposits, balances, and dates.
  • Payments Records individual transactional payments made against registrations.
  • Lessons Schedules theoretical & practical sessions connecting registrations, instructors, and vehicles.

⚙️ Assets

  • Vehicles Stores vehicle registry, types (sedan, SUV, truck), and maintenance statuses.
